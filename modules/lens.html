Functions

focus (num_steps[, step_size=1[, wait=1[, extra_delay=0]]])
Moves the focus motor a specified number of steps. Only works in LV.

Parameters:

  • num_steps integer
  • step_size integer (default 1)
  • wait integer (default 1)
  • extra_delay integer (default 0)
